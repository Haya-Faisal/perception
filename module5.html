Overview

Course description

This is a first step to self-discovery module. It introduces students to who they are, their relationship with Allah, and how Islam guides every aspect of their daily life.

Module 1 introduces Islamic theology and scripture with topics such as "What is Islam", "Introduction to the Quran" and "Beautiful Names of Allah (SWT)". The material emphasises a believer's relationship with Allah and her interactions with those around her.

Short Quranic Surahs are introduced through innovative activities. Surah Al Bayyinah's key message is explored through a 'building a house' exercise that makes the lesson memorable and fun.

There is a strong component of personal development with topics such as the "90/10 Principle", "How to have a Beautiful Mind" and "Finding Courage." Deep-rooted spiritual diseases such as greed and lying are also discussed.

Learning outcomes

  • Understand the concept of Tauheed (Oneness of God)
  • Connect how logic leads to Tauheed
  • Understand how Tauheed manifests in daily life, celebrations and entertainment
  • Appreciate Islam as a way of life — not just a set of religious rituals
  • Recognise the meaning of being Allah (SWT)'s viceroys on Earth
  • Comprehend the sync between Islam and science
  • Categorise types of lies, their implications, and learn how to overcome them
  • Differentiate between needs, wants and greed
